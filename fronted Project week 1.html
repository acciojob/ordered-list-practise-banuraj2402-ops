Introducing the Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small to medium-sized hound, known for its strong scent-detection abilities. They are known to be curious, gentle, and generally happy dogs, making them popular family pets.

Beagle Trivia

  1. Scent Power: They possess one of the best senses of smell of any dog breed.
  2. Origin: The modern Beagle was developed in **Great Britain**.
  3. Name Meaning: The name is thought to have come from the Old French word begle, meaning small.
  4. Temperament: They are often described as merry and active hounds.
